DINOSAURS

Dinos basically ran the earth for millions of years. they were just massive reptiles—some were huge plant-eaters and others were fast predators. a giant asteroid wiped almost all of them out about 66 million years ago. the crazy part is a few survived and actually evolved into the birds we see today.

About different dinosaurs

The triceratops was basically a living tank. it had three massive horns and a giant bony shield around its neck. they spent their days just eating plants, but they were definitely built for a fight since they lived in the exact same neighborhood as the t-rex.

As for velociraptors, they weren't actually as big as the movies make them look—they were closer to the size of a large turkey. but they were fast, smart, and had nasty claws. plus, we now know they were covered in feathers, making them look like incredibly dangerous ground birds.

The stegosaurus is the one with those big plates down its back and a spiked tail. they were huge, slow plant-eaters with a brain about the size of a walnut. those plates on their back were probably just to show off or control their body temp, but that spiked tail was definitely for smashing anything that tried to attack them.

Brachiosaurus was one of those giant long-necks. they were basically walking mountains that spent all day just vacuuming up leaves from the tops of trees. they were so massive that once they were fully grown, they probably didn't even care about predators because nothing was big enough to mess with them.
